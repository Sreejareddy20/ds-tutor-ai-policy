Account & Data Deletion

At DS Tutor AI, you have total control over your information. We provide two ways to permanently delete your account and history.

Option 1: In-App Automatic Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the app and log in.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Tap "Delete Account."

Effect: Your profile and entire chat history will be instantly erased from our cloud database.

Option 2: Email Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at:
dstutorai@gmail.com

We will manually purge your personal data and chat logs within 7 business days.
